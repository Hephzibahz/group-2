What is it?

Exam Professor is a web-based tool that allows you to build, embed, sell access to, and manage your own exams, tests or quizzes (or drill and practice) quickly and easily.

From professors setting up their final exam to small businesses training employees, Exam Professor is simple, powerful and a breeze to use.

Sign-up in two minutes

Exam Professor is offered in five plans that range from FREE (which only lets you manage 1 exam) to $12 / month up to $99 / month for unlimited exams. All paying plans include a 14-day free trial.

Simply pay by the month with your credit card, then change plans or even cancel your account at any time.

Exam Professor is a feature rich, fully responsive, exam management engine.
