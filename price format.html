  • Are there discounts for annual service plans?

    You bet! We currently offer 10% off 1 year commitments and 30% off on two year commitments. There are no refunds on annual service contracts.


    How long are your contracts?

    Unless you sign up for one of our annual plans, all Exam Professor plans are month to month and you may cancel at any time. It's easy to stop your service at any time.


    Can I get invoiced instead of paying through a credit card?

    Absolutely. Just contact support to get that set up.


    Can we customize Exam Professor?

    Of course! You can choose colors, upload your own logo, and set the size of your exams. And, just like a YouTube video you can embed your exams (or the student interface) into your own web pages. Everything will look like your own custom built solution.


    Can I add additional teachers?

    Yes! With Exam Professor's pro, business, and ultimate plans you can add additional teachers to your account. We have additional information on how multiple teacher accounts work on our blog.


    Please explain plan limits?

    The number of students and exams you can add to your account is based on plan type. There are no limits to the number of students that can self-register for paying exams. Also, there are no limits to the number of times your students can take and retake your exams.


    Can I get my data out if I want to discontinue using Exam Professor?

    Yes, you can easily export all your reports, students and exam data as an Adobe PDF or Excel document at any time.


    If we sign up half-way through a month, will we be charged the full amount?

    No. We will not bill your account until day 14 from the day you sign-up. Your first billing date will remain the same each month. You can cancel at any time.


    If we cancel, will you refund our current months charges?

    No. We do not issue refunds for payments that have already been taken. If you want to cancel, make sure to do it before the day of the month you are initially signed up so you do not get charged.


    What happens if we upgrade, downgrade, or cancel in the middle of a monthly billing cycle?

    If you upgrade or downgrade, you will be charged the new rate for your new plan starting on your next billing cycle. If you cancel, you will be canceled immediately and you won't be charged again.


    What happens if we upgrade, downgrade, or cancel in the middle of an annual billing cycle?

    We offer substantial discounts to those willing to commit to Exam Professor long term. If you change your billing cycle back to monthly, your remaining balance will be awarded as a credit. There are no refunds for annual accounts, so make sure you are willing to commit long term before signing up for our annual or biennial plans.


    Can I get personal help?

    Absolutely! When you login to Exam Professor you should immediately know where to go, what to do, and how to get it done without complex direction or diagrams. However, if you do need help you can contact us through our built in support system, email, or by phone at your convenience.
